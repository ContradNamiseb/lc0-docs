Detailed Description

Garbage collector for nodes in the DAG.

Deferring deletion of nodes prevents race conditions with other threads that might be reading from them during tree reuse or transposition. The collector runs in a separate thread and wakes up periodically or when the queue is full to delete nodes that are no longer reachable. It uses a double-buffering scheme with thread-local queues and a shared queue to minimize contention.
