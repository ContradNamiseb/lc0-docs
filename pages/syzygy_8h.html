Detailed Description

Syzygy endgame tablebase interface.

This file provides the interface for loading and probing Syzygy endgame tablebases. Syzygy tablebases encode perfect endgame information for positions with up to 7 pieces. The tablebases support:

  • WDL (Win/Draw/Loss): Quick determination of theoretical outcome.
  • DTZ (Distance To Zeroing move): Number of moves until a pawn move or capture (zeroing the 50-move counter) under optimal play.
