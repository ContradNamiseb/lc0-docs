Detailed Description

Represents a chess board position in the Lc0 engine.

This class manages the state of a chess board including piece placement, castling rights, en passant information, and game state. Unlike most chess engines, the board is internally mirrored when it's black's turn to move, so the "us" (current player) is always white from the internal perspective.
